I am from Winnipeg, Manitoba. Prior to moving east to Ottawa, I attended the University of Manitoba and completed a Bachelor’s of Environmental Design in the Landscape + Urbanism stream and a Master’s of Landscape Architecture. Since completing my landscape architecture studies I worked in both Saskatoon, SK, and Winnipeg, MB, at different landscape architecture firms.
